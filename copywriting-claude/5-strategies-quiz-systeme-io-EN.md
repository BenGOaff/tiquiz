## Strategy 1 — Align Your Quiz with Your Existing Systeme.io Offers

The classic mistake: building a quiz "because it's trendy" without thinking about what you'll sell after. You capture 300 leads, end up with 300 contacts and no clear offer to push, and three weeks later your list is back to silence.

Your **Systeme.io quiz** should be designed as **the entry point to the funnels you already run**. Every result profile maps to an offer you already have in Systeme.io, or to a maturity tier that leads toward it.

### The 3-step method

**1. List your current Systeme.io offers.** Open your dashboard, look at your active funnels, write down each offer with its price and target buyer. You probably have between 2 and 5.

**2. Identify the buyer profile for each offer.** For every one, answer: who buys this? What are they looking for? What's their main pain point? What's their experience level?

**3. Build a quiz with one result profile per offer.** 4 offers = 4 profiles. Each quiz answer steers the prospect toward the profile that matches their current need, which leads them directly to the most relevant offer.

### Concrete example

Let's say you sell, through Systeme.io:

- A $47 course (beginners)
- A $297 group program (intermediates)
- A $1,497 one-on-one coaching package (advanced)

Your quiz "What kind of entrepreneur are you?" auto-segments into 3 profiles. The beginner lands on a result page that pre-sells the $47 course. The intermediate sees the one pre-selling the $297 program. The advanced sees the 1-to-1. Each tag dropped into Systeme.io kicks off the matching email sequence.

## Strategy 3 — Write a Headline That Stops the Scroll

The **quiz headline** is your storefront. Get it wrong, and nothing else matters — your prospect won't even click.

A solid **Systeme.io quiz headline** checks three boxes:

- **Who you're talking to** (the target profile is visible in 2-3 words)
- **What they'll discover** (the result promise)
- **The emotional hook** (curiosity, challenge, self-assessment)

### 7 headline patterns that convert in 2026

1. **"What kind of [profile] are you?"** → classic, universal
2. **"Are you ready for [outcome]?"** → direct challenge
3. **"What's really blocking your [goal]?"** → puts a finger on the pain
4. **"In X months, where will your [area] be?"** → projection, curiosity
5. **"The 2-minute test that tells you if [benefit]"** → time-saver + benefit
6. **"Is your [area] (really) ready to [outcome]?"** → honest doubt
7. **"Which offer should you launch first to [outcome]?"** → decision helper

You can test 2-3 headlines in parallel using Systeme.io's **A/B split pages**, or just duplicate your quiz. Whichever one wins on click-through rate, keep it.

## Strategy 4 — Ask Questions That Segment Your Systeme.io Contacts

A quiz that segments is a quiz where **every question applies a filter**. By the end, each lead is classified into a specific profile, and each profile fires a specific tag in your Systeme.io.

### The 5 rules for segmenting questions

**1. One question = one piece of segmentation.** Every question should let you classify your prospect on ONE dimension (budget, level, urgency, goal...). Don't cram two things in one.

**2. 5 to 8 questions, no more.** Past that, completion rate crashes. Under 5, you're not segmenting finely enough for your Systeme.io tags to be useful.

**3. Answer options that map to your tags.** If you have 4 profiles in your Systeme.io (beginner, motivated, pragmatic, advanced), every question needs 4 options that map to those 4 profiles.

**4. Concrete over theoretical.** Prefer "When you look at tomorrow's calendar, what do you feel?" over "How do you evaluate your relationship with professional time?". Your prospect answers instinctively, not intellectually.

**5. No questions that hurt.** Skip "How many times have you failed a launch?" — swap for "What's holding you back right now?". Same info, more dignified. Your prospect stays.

### The direct link to your Systeme.io automations

Each result profile should trigger inside your Systeme.io:

- **A specific tag** (`profile-beginner`, `profile-motivated`, etc.)
- **Automatic enrollment in the matching course** (`sio_course_id`)
- **Addition to the profile's community** (`sio_community_id`)
- **A dedicated email sequence** (fired by the tag)

Which means your entire Systeme.io funnel starts running AUTOMATICALLY based on the profile detected by the quiz. Never another generic email blasted to the whole list.
